# Strategy Analysis Framework

Reference framework for scoring and analyzing competitor YouTube video strategy across five dimensions.

---

## 1. Title Analysis Framework

### Formula Patterns to Detect

| Pattern | Example | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| "How to..." | "How to Build an AI Agent in 10 Minutes" | Tutorial/educational signal |
| "X Ways..." / "X Things..." | "7 Ways AI Will Replace Your Job" | Listicle — sets clear expectations |
| "I [verb]..." | "I Built a $10K/month AI Business" | Personal story — high curiosity |
| "Why..." | "Why Most AI Tools Are Useless" | Contrarian — triggers disagreement |
| "Stop [verb]ing..." | "Stop Using ChatGPT Wrong" | Command — pattern interrupt |
| "The [noun] that..." | "The AI Tool That Changed Everything" | Mystery — curiosity gap |
| "What [noun] taught me..." | "What 100 Days of AI Taught Me" | Experience-based authority |
| "[Number] + [Timeframe]" | "I Made $5K in 30 Days With AI" | Concrete proof — high click rate |
| "You're [verb]ing ... wrong" | "You're Using Notion Wrong" | Challenge — ego hook |
| "[Noun] vs [Noun]" | "Claude vs ChatGPT — The Real Winner" | Comparison — decision intent |

### Power Word Categories

- **Numbers**: Specific figures, percentages, timeframes (e.g., "10x", "$5K", "30 days")
- **Emotional triggers**: "Secret", "Shocking", "Nobody tells you", "Truth about"
- **Urgency**: "Now", "2026", "Before it's too late", "Still not using"
- **Curiosity gaps**: "This one thing", "The real reason", "What happened when"

### Title Length Scoring

| Length | Score | Rationale |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| 40-60 characters | ✅ Strong | Optimal for YouTube search + mobile display |
| 30-39 characters | ⚠️ Moderate | May lack specificity |
| 61-80 characters | ⚠️ Moderate | Gets truncated on mobile |
| <30 or >80 characters | ❌ Weak | Too vague or too long |

### Overall Title Scoring

- **✅ Strong (3+ elements)**: Clear formula + power words + optimal length + curiosity trigger
- **⚠️ Medium (2 elements)**: Has formula but lacks emotional pull, or good length but generic wording
- **❌ Weak (0-1 elements)**: No clear formula, no power words, poor length

## 2. Hook Analysis Framework (First 30 Seconds / ~200 Words)

### Hook Type Classification

| Type | Description | Example Opening |
|------|-------------|-----------------|
| **Question** | Opens with a direct question to the viewer | "Bạn có biết tại sao 90% người dùng AI thất bại?" |
| **Shocking stat** | Leads with a surprising data point | "95% of AI projects fail in the first 6 months." |
| **Story/anecdote** | Personal narrative or case study opening | "3 tháng trước, mình gần như bỏ cuộc..." |
| **Live demo** | Shows the result or tool immediately | "Nhìn này — mình vừa tạo một website trong 2 phút." |
| **Bold claim** | Makes a provocative statement | "AI Agents sẽ thay thế 80% công việc văn phòng." |
| **Myth bust** | Challenges a common belief | "Mọi người nghĩ cần biết code để dùng AI. Sai." |

### Time-to-Value Scoring

| Speed | Score | Description |
|-------|-------|-------------|
| < 5 seconds | ✅ Immediate | First insight or proof delivered in opening line |
| 5-15 seconds | ⚠️ Moderate | Brief context before first value point |
| > 15 seconds | ❌ Slow | Long intro, channel branding, or throat-clearing before value |

### Retention Signals to Identify

- **Pattern interrupt**: Unexpected visual/audio change in first 5 seconds
- **Visual hook vs. Audio hook**: Does the hook rely on what you SEE or what you HEAR?
- **Promise specificity**: Vague ("I'll show you something cool") vs. Specific ("You'll learn 3 automation workflows")
- **Open loop**: Creates a question the viewer needs answered ("...but what happened next changed everything")
- **Social proof**: Mentions results, numbers, or credentials early

## 3. Video Structure Framework

### Structure Type Classification

| Type | Flow | Best For |
|------|------|----------|
| **Linear** | A → B → C (sequential progression) | Tutorials, step-by-step guides |
| **Problem-Solution** | Pain → Agitate → Solve | Product demos, tool reviews |
| **Listicle** | Point 1, Point 2, ... Point N | Tips, tools, mistakes |
| **Tutorial/Step-by-step** | Step 1 → Step 2 → ... → Result | How-to content |
| **Story-driven** | Setup → Conflict → Resolution | Personal journey, case studies |
| **Hybrid** | Combines 2+ types | Long-form educational content |

### Section Analysis Checklist

- [ ] **Total section count**: How many distinct segments?
- [ ] **Intro length**: Time before main content starts (target: < 60 seconds)
- [ ] **Average section length**: Consistent or variable pacing?
- [ ] **Pacing variation**: Does the energy/speed change between sections?
- [ ] **Transitions**: How does the creator move between sections? (verbal cue, visual, none)
- [ ] **Conclusion length**: How long is the wrap-up? Does it feel rushed or drawn out?

### CTA Placement Analysis

| Placement | Position | Effectiveness |
|-----------|----------|---------------|
| **Early** | First 25% of video | ⚠️ Can feel pushy — works if value is front-loaded |
| **Middle** | 25-75% of video | ✅ Natural break point — high conversion if earned |
| **End** | Last 25% of video | ⚠️ Low reach (most viewers drop off) but high intent |
| **Multiple** | 2+ CTAs spread throughout | ✅ Best coverage — if each feels natural, not repetitive |

### Engagement Hooks Within the Video

- **Mid-roll hooks**: "But here's where it gets interesting..." / "The next one is the most important"
- **Segment transitions**: Numbered lists, visual chapter markers, verbal signposts
- **Cliffhangers**: Teasing upcoming content to prevent drop-off
- **Interactive prompts**: "Comment below if you...", "Pause and try this"

## 4. Thumbnail Analysis Framework (via MiniMax understand_image)

### Visual Elements Checklist

| Element | Check | Details |
|---------|-------|---------|
| **Face presence** | Yes / No / Partial | Faces with expressions increase CTR by ~30% |
| **Text overlay** | Amount (0-3 words ideal) | Readable at small size? Complements or repeats title? |
| **Color contrast** | High / Medium / Low | Does it pop against YouTube's white/dark backgrounds? |
| **Composition** | Rule of thirds, centered, asymmetric | Where does the eye land first? |
| **Emotion conveyed** | Surprise, curiosity, excitement, authority | Does the facial expression match the title's energy? |
| **Brand elements** | Logo, consistent colors, recurring style | Can you identify the channel from thumbnail alone? |

### Thumbnail Style Classification

| Style | Description | Best For |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Clean/minimal** | 1-2 elements, lots of whitespace, bold text | Authority/educational channels |
| **Busy/clickbait** | Multiple elements, arrows, circles, emojis | Entertainment/reaction content |
| **Screenshot/demo** | Shows tool or result directly | Tutorial/tech channels |
| **Before/after** | Split comparison | Transformation content |
| **Face + text** | Close-up face with 2-3 word overlay | General high-CTR format |

### Cross-Thumbnail Consistency

When analyzing multiple thumbnails from the same channel:
- Do they use a consistent color palette?
- Is there a recognizable layout template?
- Does the text style (font, size, placement) repeat?
- Would a viewer recognize this channel's thumbnails in a feed?

## 5. Cross-Video Pattern Matrix

### Format Frequency Analysis

For batch analysis (5+ videos from one channel), build a frequency table:

```
| Format Type       | Count | % of Total | Avg Views | vs. Channel Avg |
|-------------------|-------|------------|-----------|-----------------|
| Tutorial          | 4     | 40%        | 15,000    | +20% ✅          |
| Listicle          | 3     | 30%        | 10,000    | -5% ⚠️          |
| Story-driven      | 2     | 20%        | 25,000    | +80% ✅          |
| Product review    | 1     | 10%        | 8,000     | -30% ❌          |
```

### Performance Correlation Signals

- **Overperformers** (>1.5x channel average views): What do they have in common?
- **Underperformers** (<0.5x channel average views): What patterns to avoid?
- **Title formula vs. views**: Which title patterns correlate with higher views?
- **Hook type vs. views**: Which hook styles drive better performance?
- **Video length vs. views**: Is there an optimal length for this channel's audience?

### Posting Cadence Analysis

- **Upload frequency**: Daily / 2-3x week / Weekly / Bi-weekly / Monthly
- **Day-of-week patterns**: Are certain days preferred?
- **Consistency score**: How regular is the schedule?
- **Gap analysis**: Are there periods of inactivity? What happened after?

### Topic Clustering

Group videos by topic and identify:
- **Core topics**: What the channel covers most (>30% of content)
- **Experimental topics**: One-off or rare content — did it perform?
- **Topic adjacency**: Which topics appear together or in sequence?
- **Trending alignment**: Do upload topics correlate with search trends?
