# Build in Public Post Creator

## Purpose
Transform raw project notes, journal entries, metrics, or session summaries into polished "build in public" (BIP) Facebook posts that build trust and social proof.

## Input
- Raw notes from a project or learning session
- Metrics and numbers (time saved, results achieved)
- Lessons learned or failures encountered
- Screenshots or tool descriptions

## Process

### Step 1: Identify BIP template
Read the input and match to the best template from `references/bip-templates.md`:
1. **Progress Update** — sharing what was built/achieved this week
2. **Failure/Lesson** — sharing a mistake and what was learned
3. **Tool/System Reveal** — revealing the tools and systems behind the scenes
4. **Milestone** — celebrating a significant achievement
5. **Behind-the-Numbers** — showing the reality behind visible results

### Step 2: Extract key elements
From the raw input, extract:
- The main story/event
- Specific numbers or metrics
- The lesson or insight
- What the audience can learn from this

### Step 3: Generate post
Write 1-2 Facebook posts following:
- Brand voice from `MY RESOURCES/BRANDVOICE.MD`
- The selected BIP template structure
- Hook > Story > Lesson > CTA pattern
- Vietnamese with English power words

## Output
1-2 Facebook posts, copy-paste ready.

## Rules
1. **Authenticity over polish** — BIP content should feel real, not scripted
2. **Include specific numbers** — "tiết kiệm 3 giờ" not "tiết kiệm thời gian"
3. **Show vulnerability** — failures and mistakes build more trust than successes
4. **Always include a lesson** — every BIP post must teach something
5. **No hype** — energy 6/10 (slightly lower than usual brand voice for authenticity)
6. **CTA to community** — end with invitation to AI Freedom Builders community

## Reference
See `references/bip-templates.md` for the 5 template structures.
